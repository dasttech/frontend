How it works

1. Save & secure Assets

You can secure your digital assets by saving it on the DAST security technology layer. It is encrypted and backed up on the blockchain. Only your wallet will be able to view or edit it. DAST will generate a recovery token that the user can hand to the next of kin or family members that can be used to recover it in case of unforeseen circumstance like lose of life.

2. Recover Assets

You or your next of kin will be able to recover your assets with the use of recovery token. Every recovery request will pass through a validation process and upon 95% positive confirmations, access will be granted.

Why Choose DAST

1. Digital Access Security Token is a working web 3 infrastructure of provable trust for all.

2. You are in full control of your digital assets, seed phrase, usernames / passwords and to your next of kin when you are no more.

3. Institutions, organizations, banks, military, businesses and individuals can secure - save, edit and restore their access to vital information on DAST platform.
